Melissa Bentley-FordPhD

A postdoctoral researcher in Dr. Tamara Caspary’s lab at Emory University (Atlanta, GA, USA). She completed her PhD at the University of Alabama at Birmingham (Birmingham, AL, USA) in the lab of Dr. Brad Yoder. Prior to that, she obtained her B.S. in Biochemistry from Kennesaw State University (Kennesaw, GA, USA). Currently, her research interests lie in the area of understanding the role of primary cilia in regulating mammalian development.
